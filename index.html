First Dua:

رَبَّنَا اغْفِرْ لِي وَلِوَالِدَيَّ وَلِلْمُؤْمِنِينَ يَوْمَ يَقُومُ الْحِسَابُ
Transliteration: Rabbana ighfir li wa li walidayya walil mumineena yawma yaqoomu alhisabu

It is translated as: “Our Lord, forgive me and my parents and the believers the Day the account
is established.”

Make Dua for my Late Father Alhaji Aliyu Alani Abdulrahim May Allah forgive all his sins,
May his grave be filled with Noor, May Jannatul Firdaus be his final abode. Amin summa Amin
(Read Suratul Iklass x3). Thank you

Second Dua:

حَسْبُنَا اللَّهُ وَ نِعْمَ الْوَ كِيلُ
Transliteration: Hasbunallah wa nimal-Wakil

It is translated as: “Sufficient for us is Allah, and [“sufficient is Allah as a guardian, a protector
and a helper”

A reminder that Allah is with you at all times, he alone you worship, and he is alone you seek
for help. As the verse in Suratul Fatiha says {إِيَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ وَإِيَّاكَ نَسْتَعِينُ} (Surah Fatiha ayat 5).

Third Dua:

اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ، وَعَلَى آلِ مُحَمَّدٍ، كَمَا صَلَّيْتَ عَلَى إِبْرَاهِيمَ وَعَلَى آلِ إِبْرَاهِيمَ، إِنَّكَ حَمِيدٌ مَجِيدٌ،
اللَّهُمَّ بَارِكْ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ، وَعَلَى آلِ مُحَمَّدٍ، كَمَا بَارَكْتَ عَلَى إِبْرَاهِيمَ، وَعَلَى آلِ إِبْرَاهِيمَ، إِنَّكَ حَمِيدٌ مَجِيدٌ
Transliteration: “Allaahumma salli ‘ala Muhammad wa ‘ala Aali Muhammad kama salayta
‘ala Ibrahim wa ‘ala aali Ibraaheem innaka hameedun majeed, Allahumma baarik
‘ala Muhammad wa ‘ala Aali Muhammad kama baarakta ‘ala Ibrahim wa ála aali
Ibraaheem innaka hameedun majeed.

It is translated as: O Allah! Send Your Mercy on Muhammad(SAW) and on the family of Muhammad(SAW),
as You sent Your Mercy on Abraham and on the family of Abraham, for You are the Most
Praise-worthy, the Most Glorious. O Allah! Send Your Blessings on Muhammad(SAW) and the
family of Muhammad(SAW), as You sent your Blessings on Abraham and on the family of Abraham,
for You are the Most Praise-worthy, the Most Glorious. (SAHIH BUKHARI)'

Fourth Dua:

رَبِّ إِنِّى لِمَآ أَنزَلۡتَ إِلَىَّ مِنۡ خَيۡرٍ فَقِيرٌ
Transliteration: Rabbi inni lima anzalta ilayya min khairin faqir

It is translated as: “My Lord, indeed I am, for whatever good You would send down to me,
in need.”

A dua made by prophet Musa (AS) in his time of need. In Suratul Qasas verse 24 we
encounter the story of Prophet Musa (AS) helping two women water their flocks
after which he made this dua.

Fifth Dua:

رَّبِّ زِدۡنِى عِلۡمًا
Transliteration: Rabbi Zidni Ilma

It is translated as: “O my Lord, Increase me in Knowledge” where Rabbi means
‘my Lord’ Zidni means ‘Increase me’ and Ilma means ‘In knowledge’.

It is often said seek Knowledge as it is a very important aspect of the
religion of Islam. May Allah make it easy on us.

Sixth Dua:

اللهم اني اسالك الجنة
Transliteration: Allahumma Inni As’aluka Al Jannah

It is translated as: O Allah, I ask You for Paradise

Anas bin Malik narrated that the Messenger of Allah (sallallahu alaihi wasallam) said:
“Whoever asks Allah (SWT) Paradise three times, Paradise says: ‘O Allah, admit him
into Paradise’, and whoever seeks refuge from the Fire three times, the Fire says:
‘O Allah, save him from the Fire.’” Al-Tirmidhi Hadith 2572

Seventh Dua:

لَّآ إِلَٰهَ إِلَّآ أَنتَ سُبْحَٰنَكَ إِنِّى كُنتُ مِنَ ٱلظَّٰلِمِينَ
Transliteration: la ilaha illa anta subhanaka inni kuntu minaz-zalimin

It is translated as: There is no deity except You; exalted are You. Indeed, I have been
of the wrongdoers.

Seeking Allah’s forgiveness whilst also knowing and acknowledging the sin one has committed
is key to a sincere repentance.

A dua made by prophet Yunus (AS), In Suratul Anbiya Verse:87 we encounter the story that led
to this dua. Seeking Allah’s forgiveness whilst also knowing and acknowledging the sin one
has committed is key to a sincere repentance.

Eight Dua:

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ عِلْمًا نَافِعًا وَرِزْقًا طَيِّبًا وَعَمَلاً مُتَقَبَّلاً
Transliteration: Allahumma inni as’aluka ‘ilman naafi’an, wa rizqan tayyiban, wa ‘amalan mutaqabbalan.

It is translated as: O Allah, I ask You for beneficial knowledge, goodly provision, and acceptable deeds.

Ninth Dua:

رَبَّنَا آتِنَا فِىْ الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً وَفِىْ الآخِرَةِ حَسَنَةً وَّقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ
Transliteration: Rabbana atina fid dunya hasanatan wa fil Aakhirati hasanatan waqina ‘azab an-nar

“Our Lord, give us in this world [that which is] good and in the Hereafter [that which is] good
and protect us from the punishment of the Fire.”

Whoever grants a person ease in this life will be rewarded by Allah abundantly
